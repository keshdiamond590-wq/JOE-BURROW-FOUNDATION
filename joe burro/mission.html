OUR MISSION

“To provide resources and support to the underprivileged and underserved.”

Thankful for the Support of Friends, Partners and Fans

Since launching in October 2022, the Joe Burrow Foundation’s mission of hunger relief and focus on youth mental health has been supported by our partners and Joe’s fans in Ohio, Louisiana, and beyond. We’re thankful for that support and the support of future partners and donors.

Click here to make a donation to help Joe and our Foundation continue to “Do Good”. Click here to join our team as a partner or volunteer.

Supporting Others in a Variety of Ways

Joe Burrow Foundation Do Good Grants totaling over $2 million have funded a variety of organizations in Ohio, Louisiana, and Kentucky that are dedicated to hunger relief and youth mental health. We are proud to support our partner organizations and the impact they make in the communities Joe has called home.

Do Good Grants are awarded twice annually to organizations who align with the Joe Burrow Foundation’s mission.
